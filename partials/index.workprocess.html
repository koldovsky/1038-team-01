Our Work Process

We are guided by clear and long-term cooperation with clients. Look through the process of our work.


01.

Research

We thoroughly research and analyze the
client's business, services, and products
to clearly understand its potential,
goals, and target audience. We use this
information for further planning.

02.

Strategy

We plan customized strategies to achieve
the maximum conversion rate. Particular
attention is paid to setting the keys to the
efficient user interface and determining
the suitable user experience.

03.

Design

We closely cooperate with our clients to
create projects that completely
demonstrate the proposed work.
Mockups are regularly improved to
satisfy all the objectives.

04.

Quality Assurance

Our professional QA team thoroughly
examines every single page to ensure that
all features perform perfectly on every
platform, bringing the most holistic user
experience for every visitor.

05.

Development

We integrate the entirely custom front-end
theme with a solid back-end foundation.
Our tailor-made solutions are the best
way to satisfy the project's goals and
provide maximum flexibility.

06.

Launch

We always do our best to offer the training
required for a smooth transition for site
administration. We provide post-launch
support to guarantee that everything runs
smoothly and fits with the client's needs.
